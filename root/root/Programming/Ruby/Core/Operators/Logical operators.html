Logical operators

  • < (less than)
  • > (greater than)
  • ≤ (greater than or equal to)
  • ≥ (less than or equal to)
  • == (equal to)
  • ≠ (not equal to)
  • ≤> (greater, equal or less - aka 'spaceship operator')
=begin
    RESULT      | MEANING
    -1          | greater than
    0           | equal to
    1           | less than
=end

puts 10 <=> 20 #=> -1
puts 40 * 2 <=> 50 * 0.5 #=> 1
