Assignment operators

  • = (equal to)
  • += (plus equal)
  • -= (less equal)
  • *= (times equal)
  • /= (divide equal)
  • **= (power equal)
  • %= (modulo equal)
  • ||= (or equal)
  • &&= (and equal)
a = 10
a %= 2
puts a #=> 0

a = 10
a **= 2
puts a #=> 100

a = 10
a ||= 10
puts a #=> 10

a = 10
a &&= 500
puts a #=> 500

=begin
    -> ||= means: if `var` doesn't exist, is false or nil, assign `value` to it.
    -> &&= means: if `var` exist and isn't false nor nil, assign `value` to it.
=end
