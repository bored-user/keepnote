Def. sing.

The definite singular form convention is the way of defining singular nouns. It basically consist in adding the noun's article at its end, creating a new (definite) noun.

For instance: “en mann” means “a man”, while “mannen” means “the man”. There are, however, irregular definite nouns, like “en jente” ("a girl"), which becomes “jenta” ("the girl"), instead of “jenteen” (which would be the regular form).
