Genetiv

Genetiv is one of the ways to express possession in Norwegian. It basically consists in adding an “s” at the end of the word (the possessor). The noun must always be in the indefinite form (independently of singular/ plural).

For instance: “Alexs bok” means “Alex's book”.

The only difference to English is the fact that Norwegian will only add an apostrophe when the word already ends with an “s”.

For instance: "Sveits' flagg" (Switzerland's flag).
