til (more)

“til” is “more” in the sense of “one more”.

If you want more of something countable, you can use the adverb "til". You then combine it with a number: “en til” becomes “one more” or “another”, “to til” becomes “two more”, and so on.

When specifying exactly what you want, by adding a noun, the number goes before the noun, while “til” goes after, like so: “en kopptil” ("one more cup").
